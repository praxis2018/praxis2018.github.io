About Us

praxis aims to increase empathy and decrease bias through research-backed VR experiences.

We pair immersive technology that exposes the experience of discrimination with engaging bias training to create sustainable change for individuals and organizations.

Services

What We Do.

.

Researched Curriculum

We provide research-backed and data-driven trainings. Walk through each training module designed specifically for diversity and bias trainings.

High-Quality Experiences

Engaging technological experiences are proven to outperform traditional methods. Our expert developers and designers create production level material.

Standardized Metrics

Along with our curriculum and training, we provide quantifiable metrics to see improved understanding and empathy of implicit bias within the workplace.

Who We Are.

We are a group of creative designers, experienced developers, and passionate leaders. We have experience in research, experience production, curriculum creation, and business development.
